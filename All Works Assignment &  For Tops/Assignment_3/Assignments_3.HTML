My Favorite Travel Destination

Paris

The Eiffel Tower, an icon of Paris and France, stands tall in the middle of the city. Located on the Champ de Mars near the Seine River, this iron structure was designed by Gustave Eiffel and completed in 1889 for the World's Fair. Visitors are captivated by its stunning architecture, panoramic views from its observation decks, and its enchanting illumination at night.
